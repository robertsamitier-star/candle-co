1.0 Executive Summary

IC Candle Co. is a direct-to-consumer candle brand specializing in custom, personalized candles designed to enhance home aesthetics and promote relaxation. Operating primarily through Shopify with multi-channel distribution across Etsy, TikTok Shop, Instagram Shop, Amazon, and an SEO-driven blog, we leverage a print-on-demand (POD) fulfillment model that eliminates inventory risk, upfront production costs, and warehousing overhead.

Our founding team of four brings complementary expertise across creative/technology, operations, e-commerce/advertising, and finance. What sets IC Candle Co. apart is our AI-powered approach: we use artificial intelligence to handle web design, SEO, copywriting, email marketing, social media management, brand strategy, and market research — services that would cost a traditional startup $11,500–$19,000 per month if outsourced to agencies. Our total monthly operating cost is approximately $40 per person.

Our goal is to achieve profitability within the first 3 months, targeting break-even at approximately 30 candle sales per month while maintaining gross margins of 43–55% depending on pricing strategy.

Key Differentiators: Zero inventory risk through POD fulfillment · AI replaces $11.5–19K/mo in agency costs · 4-person team with zero role overlap · Total launch cost ~$830 (~$208 per person) · Break-even at ~30 candles.

2.0 Company Overview

Mission Statement

To brighten homes with clean-burning, thoughtfully curated scents that provide a moment of peace — delivered through a modern, AI-driven e-commerce experience with zero waste and zero inventory risk.

Business Structure

LLC registered in the State of Florida. The company operates as a 4-member limited liability company with clearly defined roles and responsibilities.

Location

Fully virtual with e-commerce operations. All production and fulfillment is handled by our POD supplier partners (primarily US-based). No physical storefront or production space required.

Core Values

  • Clean Ingredients — Coconut apricot wax (a vegan blend of natural apricot, coconut, and soy), cotton wicks (lead and zinc free) and FSC-certified wood wicks, natural wax color with no added dyes

  • American Made — Every candle hand-poured in the USA using domestically sourced coconut apricot wax and fragrance oils. Recyclable glass, tin, and crystal vessels
  • Transparency — Full ingredient disclosure on every listing, honest marketing, clear return and exchange policies
  • Innovation — AI-first marketing and operations, enabling a lean 4-person team to compete with funded brands at near-zero marketing cost

The global scented candle market continues to grow, driven by a strong consumer shift towards sustainability, wellness-focused aromas, and personalized products. 6.0 Operational Plan

Print-on-Demand Model

IC Candle Co. operates a print-on-demand (POD) fulfillment model. When a customer places an order, it automatically routes to our supplier partner who hand-pours the candle with our custom label and ships directly to the customer under our brand. We never touch inventory.

How It Works: Customer Orders → Auto-Routed to Supplier → Produced (hand-poured with our label) → Shipped Directly to Customer → We Collect the Margin. You only pay the supplier after the customer pays you.

Primary Supplier: Candle Bliss

  • 20+ scent options, coconut/apricot wax blend
  • Free Shopify and Etsy app integrations — automated fulfillment
  • Phoenix, AZ fulfillment center — 1–3 day turnaround
  • White-label only, strong gift packaging options
  • Free to join, no minimum order quantity
  • Prop 65 compliant, phthalate-free fragrance

Secondary Supplier: Printed Mint

  • Best gift packaging (ribbons, crinkle paper, custom inserts)
  • Broad marketplace reach via Order Desk integration
  • Limited to 4 scents, 4–7 day fulfillment, manual record keeping
  • Best for gift-focused niche requiring premium unboxing

Tertiary Supplier: Candle Builders

  • Fast turnaround (1–2 days), New Hampshire fulfillment center
  • Native Shopify and Etsy integrations, 8+ scent options
  • White-label gift packaging, soy wax only
  • Free to join, no MOQ — good speed-focused backup supplier
  • Limited vessel variety (3 glass types)

Shipping Strategy

  • Standard: Pass shipping cost to customer
  • "Free shipping": Build shipping into retail price
  • Threshold: Free shipping on 2+ candle orders
  • Candle Bliss: Free shipping at $99+ order value

Risk Management

Chargeback Risk: As seller of record, IC Candle Co. is named first in any customer dispute — not the supplier. Every order must be documented with tracking number, fulfillment confirmation, and customer communications. Chargebacks without documentation are almost always lost.

Returns Policy:

  • POD suppliers do NOT accept returns on custom items
  • Damaged or wrong orders → replacement shipped, no return required
  • Customer changed mind → store credit or exchange
  • Personalized items → final sale (clearly stated on all listings)
  • Budget 2–3% of revenue as returns/replacement buffer

Insurance:

  • General Liability + Product Liability Insurance required (~$300–600/year)
  • Recommended providers: Next Insurance, Thimble, Hiscox
  • Coverage must be secured BEFORE first sale
  • Review supplier terms to understand where their liability ends

Record Keeping:

  • Log every order: tracking #, fulfillment date, destination
  • Screenshot customer communications when issues arise
  • Use platform dashboards (Candle Bliss/Printify auto-track)
  • For Printed Mint — supplement with manual records
  • Records are primary defense in any payment dispute

Break-Even Analysis

With monthly overhead of approximately $140 (excluding ad spend) and an average net margin of $12–$23 per unit:

  • Conservative scenario ($12/unit): Break-even at ~12 candles/month (overhead only) or ~30 candles/month (including ad spend at $200/mo)
  • Strong scenario ($23/unit): Break-even at ~7 candles/month (overhead only) or ~15 candles/month (including ad spend)
