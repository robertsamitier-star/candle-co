02 / 17 Overview

Executive Summary

IC Candle Co. is a direct-to-consumer candle brand powered by AI and fulfilled through print-on-demand. We sell custom, personalized candles online with zero inventory and near-zero operating costs.

Mission

To brighten homes with clean-burning, thoughtfully curated scents that provide a moment of peace — delivered through a modern, AI-driven e-commerce experience with zero waste and zero inventory risk.

The Model

Customers order personalized candles from our Shopify storefront. Orders auto-route to our POD supplier who hand-pours and ships directly under our brand. AI handles all marketing, copy, SEO, and email — replacing $11K–19K/mo in agency costs.

Core Values

Sustainability, transparency in ingredients, premium quality. All candles use plant-based wax, phthalate-free fragrance, and recyclable vessels.

Key Advantage

4-person team, each with domain expertise. AI replaces an entire marketing department. We launch for ~$830 total (~$208/person) and break even at just 30 candle sales.

04 / 17 Model

How the Model Works

Print-on-demand fulfillment — you only pay after the customer pays you.

01 Customer Orders Buyer places order on your Shopify or Etsy store
→
02 Auto-Routed Order routes automatically to POD supplier via app integration
→
03 Produced Supplier hand-pours candle with your label applied
→
04 Shipped Ships directly to customer — fully under your brand
→
05 You Profit You collect the margin. Zero inventory, zero touchpoints

Key Insight

You only pay the supplier after the customer pays you. The difference between your retail price and supplier cost is your margin. Zero inventory risk, zero upfront product cost.

06 / 17 Market

Market Analysis

Industry Trends (2026)

The global scented candle market is growing, with a strong shift towards sustainability and wellness-focused aromas. Consumers increasingly seek clean-burning, non-toxic options with premium ingredients.

Target Market

Individuals aged 25–40, primarily homeowners or apartment dwellers who prioritize interior décor and self-care routines. Eco-conscious professionals seeking premium, personalized products.

Competitive Advantage

Unlike mass-market brands, IC Candle Co. offers unique, artisanal scent profiles with premium non-toxic wax blends — delivered via a lean POD model that eliminates inventory risk and enables rapid iteration.

Why POD + AI Wins

Traditional candle startups carry $3K–10K in raw materials inventory, require production space, and spend months iterating. Our POD model launches in weeks with ~$830 total investment. AI handles all brand, marketing, and content creation — letting us move at the speed of a tech startup while selling a physical product.

17 / 17 Risk

Risk Management

⚠️

Chargeback Risk

You are the seller of record. If a customer disputes a charge, YOU are named first — not the supplier. Document every order with tracking number, fulfillment confirmation, and customer communications. Chargebacks without documentation are almost always lost.

↩️

Returns

  • Suppliers do NOT accept returns on POD items
  • Damaged/wrong → replace, no return required
  • Customer changed mind → store credit or exchange
  • Personalized items → final sale (state on listing)
  • Budget 2–3% of revenue as returns buffer
🛡️

Insurance

  • You carry exposure as the visible brand
  • Get General + Product Liability (~$300–600/yr)
  • Providers: Next Insurance, Thimble, Hiscox
  • Read supplier terms — know where liability ends
  • Get coverage BEFORE your first sale
📋

Record Keeping

  • Log every order: tracking #, fulfillment date, destination
  • Screenshot customer comms if issues arise
  • Use platform dashboards (Printify/Candle Bliss)
  • For Printed Mint — supplement with manual records
  • Records are your defense in any payment dispute
