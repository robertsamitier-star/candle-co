02 / 17 Overview

Executive Summary

IC Candle Co. is a direct-to-consumer candle brand powered by AI and fulfilled through print-on-demand. We sell custom, personalized candles online with zero inventory and near-zero operating costs.

Mission

To brighten homes with clean-burning, thoughtfully curated scents that provide a moment of peace — delivered through a modern, AI-driven e-commerce experience with zero waste and zero inventory risk.

The Model

Two revenue streams powered by Printify's multi-vendor platform. Stream 1: Our curated IC Candle Co. branded line across multiple vendors. Stream 2: Custom candle service — customers submit imagery, we AI-style it (caricatures, comic book, cartoon, superhero, niche themes) into unique labels. All orders auto-route through Printify. AI handles all marketing — replacing $11K–19K/mo in agency costs.

Core Values

Sustainability, transparency in ingredients, premium quality. All candles use plant-based wax, phthalate-free fragrance, and recyclable vessels.

Key Advantage

4-person team, each with domain expertise. AI replaces an entire marketing department. We launch for ~$1,185 total (~$296/person) and break even at just 30 candle sales.

Brand Name

✓ Amber & Essence Candle Co.

Alternates: Cinder & Sage · Cinder & Lore · Cinder & Bloom · Vesper & Vine · Noir & Nectar

How the Model Works

→

06 / 17 Market

Market Analysis

Industry Trends (2026)

The global scented candle market is growing, with a strong shift towards sustainability and wellness-focused aromas. Consumers increasingly seek clean-burning, non-toxic options with premium ingredients.

Target Market

Individuals aged 25–40, primarily homeowners or apartment dwellers who prioritize interior décor and self-care routines. Eco-conscious professionals seeking premium, personalized products.

Competitive Advantage

Unlike mass-market brands, IC Candle Co. offers unique, artisanal scent profiles with premium non-toxic wax blends — delivered via a lean POD model that eliminates inventory risk and enables rapid iteration.

Why POD + AI Wins

Traditional candle startups carry $3K–10K in raw materials inventory, require production space, and spend months iterating. Our POD model launches in weeks with ~$1,185 total investment. AI handles all brand, marketing, and content creation — letting us move at the speed of a tech startup while selling a physical product.

17 / 17 Risk

Risk Management

⚠️

Chargeback Risk

You are the seller of record. If a customer disputes a charge, YOU are named first — not the supplier. Document every order with tracking number, fulfillment confirmation, and customer communications. Chargebacks without documentation are almost always lost.

↩️

Returns

  • Suppliers do NOT accept returns on POD items
  • Damaged/wrong → replace, no return required
  • Customer changed mind → store credit or exchange
  • Personalized items → final sale (state on listing)
  • Budget 2–3% of revenue as returns buffer
🛡️

Insurance

  • You carry exposure as the visible brand
  • Get General + Product Liability (~$300–600/yr)
  • Providers: Next Insurance, Thimble, Hiscox
  • Read supplier terms — know where liability ends
  • Get coverage BEFORE your first sale
📋

Record Keeping

  • Log every order: tracking #, fulfillment date, destination
  • Screenshot customer comms if issues arise
  • Use Printify dashboard for all order tracking
  • Records are your defense in any payment dispute

8. Risk Management & Operations

Chargeback Risk

Critical: As seller of record, IC Candle Co. is named first in any customer dispute — not the supplier. Every order must be documented with tracking number, fulfillment confirmation, and customer communications. Chargebacks without documentation are almost always lost.

Returns Policy

  • POD suppliers do NOT accept returns on custom items
  • Damaged or wrong orders → replacement shipped, no return required
  • Customer changed mind → store credit or exchange
  • Personalized items → final sale (clearly stated on all listings)
  • Budget 2–3% of revenue as returns/replacement buffer

Insurance Requirements

  • General Liability + Product Liability Insurance required (~$300–600/year)
  • Recommended providers: Next Insurance, Thimble, Hiscox
  • Coverage must be secured BEFORE first sale
  • Review supplier terms to understand where their liability ends

Record Keeping

  • Log every order: tracking #, fulfillment date, destination
  • Screenshot customer communications when issues arise
  • Use Printify dashboard for all order tracking
  • Records are primary defense in any payment dispute

Shipping Strategy

  • Standard: Pass shipping cost to customer
  • "Free shipping": Build shipping into retail price
  • Threshold: Free shipping on 2+ candle orders
  • Printify: Volume discounts available on bulk

Custom Order Policies

Important: Custom/personalized candles are final sale. Clearly state on all listings. Turnaround time: 3–5 business days for design + 2–5 days fulfillment.

  • Custom orders are non-refundable — state prominently on product page and checkout
  • Provide design proof to customer for approval before production
  • Allow one round of revisions per order at no additional charge
  • Set clear expectations: "Design: 3–5 business days. Fulfillment: 2–5 business days. Total: 5–10 business days."
