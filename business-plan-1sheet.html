Executive Summary

IC Candle Co. is a direct-to-consumer candle brand with two revenue streams: a curated branded candle line and a custom candle service powered by AI image styling. Operating through Printify's multi-vendor platform with Shopify as our primary storefront, we leverage print-on-demand fulfillment that eliminates inventory risk and enables premium margins.

Our AI-powered approach handles marketing, design, copywriting, and image styling — services costing $14,500–24,000/month if outsourced. Our custom candle service transforms customer photos into stylized artwork (caricatures, comic book, cartoon, superhero, and niche-specific styles) printed as unique candle labels, commanding 65–75% margins.

Key Differentiators

  • Zero inventory risk through POD fulfillment via Printify
  • AI replaces $14.5–24K/mo in agency + design costs
  • Dual revenue: branded line (43–55% margin) + custom service (65–75% margin)
  • 4-person team with zero role overlap
  • Total launch cost ~$885 (~$221 per person)
  • Break-even at ~30 candles/month

Branded Candle Line

Custom Candle Service

Customer submits image → Selects AI style → We transform artwork → Label designed → Printify fulfills → Premium delivered

Custom Candle Service

Customers submit personal photos. Our AI transforms them into 5 style options — Caricatures, Comic Book, Cartoon, Superhero, and Niche-Specific designs (memorial portraits, pet illustrations, wedding art). Custom labels printed via Printify. Retail: $45–65+. Margins: 65–75%. Design cost: ~$0 (AI).
